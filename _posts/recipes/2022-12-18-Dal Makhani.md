title: Dal Makhani
category: recipe
---

A classic with both chapati and rice. 

---
{: data-content=" Ingredients "}

- black beans
- 2 onions
- 2 tomatoes
- 2 pods garlic
- half-inch ginger
- oil
- 5-6 lavang[^1]
- 2 bay leaves[^2]
- pinch jeera[^3]
- 5-6 elaichi[^4]
- 1 tbsp garam masala[^5]
- 1 tbsp chili powder[^6]
- 1 tsp haldi[^7]
- 2-3 big pinches casuri methi[^8]
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
