Easy chutney for dosa.

---
{: data-content=" Ingredients "}

- 2 onions
- dry chinch[^1]
- 1 green[^2] and 2 red[^3] chilies
- 1 tablespoon urad dal[^4]
- 1 teaspoon jeera[^5]
- 1 teaspoon mohra[^6]
- salt
