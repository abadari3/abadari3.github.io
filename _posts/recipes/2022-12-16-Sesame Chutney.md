title: Sesame Chutney

Easy chutney for dosa.

---
{: data-content=" Ingredients "}

- 3 red chilies[^1]
- 1/2 teaspoon hing[^2]
- 1.5 cups teel (sesame seeds)
- soaked chinch[^3]
- gud (jaggery)[^4]
- mohra[^5], jeera[^6], karepak for ponhi [^7]
- salt
