An essential for almost every recipe. 

---
{: data-content=" Ingredients "}

- 1 tsp methi[^1]
- 1 tsp mohra[^2]
- 3 green chilies[^3]
- 3 red chilies[^4]
- 1 stalk of karepak
- oil
