title: Peach Cobbler
category: recipe
---

Classic southern dessert from Boy Scouts. 

---
{: data-content=" Ingredients "}


- 0.25 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp corn starch
- 2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 0.25 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup milk
- canned peaches 7 oz.
- 1 cup self rising flour
- 1.25 cups sugar 
- 1 stick unsalted butter 
- *optional* vanilla ice cream
