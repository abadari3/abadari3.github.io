title: Ambatbhaji

One of my favorites. 

---
{: data-content=" Ingredients "}

- 1.5 cups of palak [^1]
- 1.5 cups of uncooked dal
- 3 cups of water (for the dal)
- 6 green chilis[^2]
- 3 red chilis[^3]
- 1 tsp haldi[^4]
- 2 tbsp of salt
- 1 tsp methi powder[^5] or methi[^6]
- A penny-sized amount of hing[^9]
- 1 stalk of karepak
- 1 inch of soaked chinch [^7]
- 1 tbsp rice flour (optional)
- mohra[^8]
- oil
