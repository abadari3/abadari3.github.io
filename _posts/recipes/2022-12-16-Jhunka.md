Quick dinner with chapati.

---
{: data-content=" Ingredients "}

- 1 onion
- 1 tomato
- 1 stalk of karepak
- 1 stalk of kothmir
- 1/2 cup chickpea flour
- tsp haldi[^1]
- tsp mohra[^2]
- tsp jeera[^3]
- 3 green chilies[^4]
- 3 red chilies[^5]
- 1/2 tsp hing[^6]
